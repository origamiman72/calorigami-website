East Bay Origami Convention 2013

The East Bay Origami Convention is an origami convention held at Barrows Hall in UC Berkeley.  In contrast to PCOC (Pacific Coast Origami Convention), our convention is on a smaller scale, but will happen every year.  Also, to be able to accommodate many origami enthusiasts in the area, admission costs are from $10-20.  We are currently doing our best to create an amazing event for the origami community.

SUBMITTING A MODEL (for display)
If you're interested in submitting a model, models do not have to be original. As long as proper documentation is provided, such as the name and author of the model, and the folder's name, you may submit any model you like.

It does not have to be part of the Theme.

CONVENTION BOOK
We are going to have a Convention Book this year! We will be accepting any diagrams, crease patterns, or origami related articles that you would like to have in the book. Please send your submissions to eboc2013@gmail.com with the subject line "Convention Book" (preferred format: pdf). The deadline will be 11/2.
