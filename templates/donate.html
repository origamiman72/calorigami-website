Donate! 
$10 will fully support a general meeting, which includes standard origami paper for all of the members. This paper will not only be used by our members, but also for community projects--University Healthworker program at Crossroads and "Destress" with Hall Association.  
$25 will fully support an advanced origami meeting, which includes larger-scale projects. Some past projects: "1000 Crane Origami for Oakland's Children Hospital", "Gigantic Modular origami", and "Wet-folding Sipho Mabona Koi". 
$50 will support publicity materials, which includes posters, flyers, brochures, banners etc. This will also help us buy origami books.
$100 will support materials for our planned convention, including pins, paper, t-shirts, etc.

Why Support Cal Origami?


1. We are the first student organization focusing on the art and aesthetics, techniques of origami, and everyday practical uses. 

2. We have a strong goal of helping others through Origami.

3. We are a growing organization at Cal, as well as one of the few fine arts on campus.

4. Due to recent budget cuts, we do not have much funds, and can only rely on donations.

5. Our organization is completely non-profit, which means every dollar we raise goes directly to essential materials to the organization.


If you'd like to donate without requesting a Tax-deductible number, you may securely donate through Paypal with a valid credit card. If you'd like to request a Tax-Deductible number, please contact Finance Chair, Jessica Yu, at calorigamidonate@gmail.com, before making the donation.
